About

N-Factor Analytical Lab was started to help people solve problems using logic and organized data. We believe that every project has a "hidden factor" that can make it run better—whether it’s a smarter spreadsheet or a cleaner workflow.

How we work

  • We listen: We learn how you currently handle your data and tasks.
  • We analyze: We use math and logic to find where things are getting stuck.
  • We build: We create easy-to-use tools in Excel and clear maps for your processes.

Our goal isn't just to give you numbers; it’s to give you peace of mind knowing that your work is organized and your path forward is clear.

Skills & Our Services

Workflow & Process Mapping

Visualizing the Way You Work

We create clear, step-by-step diagrams (BPMN) that show exactly how your business tasks flow from start to finish. This helps everyone on your team understand their role and helps find ways to make work move faster.

Spreadsheet & Data Management

Organizing Your Information

We turn messy files into clean, smart spreadsheets. Whether it’s building custom trackers in Excel or automating repetitive data tasks, we make sure your information is organized, accurate, and easy to use.

Data & Math Modeling

Solving Problems with Logic

We use math to look at your data and find patterns. By creating simple models, we can help you predict future trends or make better decisions based on facts and numbers rather than just guessing.

MATLAB & C++

Strong foundation for problem solving

We apply MATLAB and C++ to build numerical solutions and prototypes for analysis, simulations, and custom tooling when needed.
